Play-Based Learning

We believe that play is the best way for children to learn and explore. Our curriculum is designed to integrate playful activities that engage the child's imagination, stimulate creativity, and encourage natural curiosity.

Healthy Nutrition

Tiny Wonderland promotes healthy eating habits. We provide nutritious snacks and meals to ensure your child receives the energy and nutrients they need for optimal growth and development.

Safety and Security

The safety of your child is our top priority. We maintain a secure facility with trained staff, proper supervision, and safety protocols in place to ensure a safe learning environment.

Why Choose Tiny Wonderland ?

Our Standard Classes

Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to meet the developmental needs of each age group

Play Group

Play Group focuses on play-based learning, where children engage in various activities, games, and social interactions to develop essential skills such as communication, fine motor skills, and socialization.

Read More

KG 1

KG1 is the subsequent stage after Play Group, In this stage, the learning process becomes more structured, and activities are designed to further develop cognitive, language, and basic numeracy skills.

Read More

KG 2

KG2 builds upon the skills and knowledge acquired in KG1 and prepares children for primary school.Teachers also emphasize preparing children for a smooth transition to formal schooling.
